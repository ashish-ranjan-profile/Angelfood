How to get free food?


Step 1. Check food expiry date and time

Step 2. Visite hotel/resturant address

Step 3. Ask the hotel/resturant manager whether the food you have posted on Angelplate is available or not.

Step 4. If the food are available then you get the free food


About usThe basic concept of this project Food Waste Management is to collect the excess/leftover food from donors such as hotels, restaurants, marriage halls , etc and distribute to the needy people .
